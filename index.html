SyncCoin ($SYNC)

SyncCoin powers the Showland ecosystem. It connects artists, playlist curators, and filmmakers through a decentralized music discovery economy.

What SyncCoin Does

  • Reward artists featured in Showland playlists
  • Unlock music submissions and premium playlist placements
  • Community voting on featured tracks
  • Support film projects using Showland music
  • Incentivize music discovery and curation

Future Ecosystem

  • Artist rewards for playlist streams
  • Community funded indie film soundtracks
  • NFT soundtrack releases
  • Token powered music discovery
